CBT

The Compulsory Basic Training (CBT) course is a requirement for anyone who wants to ride a motorcycle or moped on public roads in the UK. The course is designed to give new riders the basic skills and knowledge they need to ride safely and confidently.

Full licence

To get a full motorcycle licence in the UK, you need to complete a CBT course, theory test, Module 1 practical test, and Module 2 practical test. The courses and tests assess your motorcycle control, road safety knowledge, and ability to deal with different traffic situations. Once you pass both Module 1 and Module 2, you'll receive a full motorcycle licence that allows you to ride any motorcycle without restrictions.
